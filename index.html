About me

Hi, I'm a Ph.D. in Computer Science from University of Cordoba (2016). I'm speciallized in computer vision. My doctoral thesis investigates the fusion of information provided by several local descriptors (natural features) and the classification of fiducial markers (artificial features) using machine learning methods.

In my spare time I enjoy playing music.


Research

  • Doctoral Thesis

    • Contributions to camera pose estimation

      Directors: Muñoz Salinas, Rafael. Medina-Carnicer, Rafael. 2016 Editor: Universidad de Córdoba, UCOPress

      The task of finding correspondences between 3D points and its 2D projections, and between 2D projections of diferent images is a fundamental step for Camera pose estimation. This PhD Thesis proposes two novel approaches to solve the problem of finding correspondeces using both natural and artificial features.
